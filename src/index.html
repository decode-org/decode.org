About Decode

Technology has become so easy to access but so easy to become a consumer of, the world has taken that mentality on. Decode is a project that tries to reverse that mentality, empowering people with technology so that they may use it and it may not use them. We do this by providing online lessons to introduce people to programming and to help them in their journey of learning it.

Decode was once a model for workshops, but there are so many great ones that we decided to focus just on lessons. Teachers can still use our resources in the classroom though. See below a TEDx talk from 2013 by Decode founder, Christian Silver about his vision for the project.
